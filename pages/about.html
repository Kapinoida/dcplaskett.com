About me!

I am interesting, hire me.

Hi! I’m David Plaskett. I enjoy technology and how it interacts with our lives on a day to day basis. From the software we interact with to the hardware that drives that interaction, I love seeing how we can incorporate technology to improve our lives and the world around us. I love to see how technology can enhance our lives and our connection with nature and the environment.

For over 20 years, I have been interested in technology and what it can hold. Being an avid PC gamer for all of that time, I have built, fixed and worked with computers in a wide variety of ways. Along with this, I have also become a practiced artist, using a mix of practical and digital media as a canvas for my creativity. I’ve come to blend the two parts of this into making headway into the web development space.

I got my Associate's degree in 2016 with a 3.97 GPA in Information Technology. I received a certificate in desktop support and am working on a bachelor’s degree with a certification in multimedia development. I have also receieve a Front-End Development Certificate from freeCodeCamp for my work in the web development world. I am working on making a career change, coming from 10 plus years in construction. It has been a difficult road, but you will not find someone more dedicated to success in web development.
